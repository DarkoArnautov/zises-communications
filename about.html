Bryan does the hard work, the long-shot work, the underdog work that topples the well-established and disrupts the status quo.  He led public finance authorities to be upgraded when Illinois peers were downgraded to junk; he ran successful bootstrapped political campaigns against the machine, and he just recently helped his wife open Chicago’s first medical cannabis dispensary – big Pharma beware. Bryan does not change opinion; he changes how people think.
